Plan Your Visit

Get the most from Lagos with practical tips and a simple checklist.

  • Use reliable local transport: danfo with caution or ride apps.
  • Visit markets early in the day for best experience.
  • Try street food from popular trusted vendors.
  • Keep a portable charger; power can fluctuate.
  • Respect local customs and ask before photographing people.
